A muscle dislocation occurs when a bone is forced out of its normal position in a joint. This can cause severe pain and immobility.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Visible deformity in the joint
  • Severe pain and swelling
  • Inability to move the joint
  • Numbness or tingling in the area

First Aid Steps

  1. Do not attempt to put the bone back in place.
  2. Keep the person calm and still.
  3. Apply ice to reduce swelling.
  4. Immobilize the joint with a splint or sling.
  5. Seek medical help immediately.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If there is visible deformity or severe pain.
  • If the person cannot move the joint.
  • If there are signs of circulation problems (numbness, tingling).

Prevention Tips

  • Warm up before physical activity.
  • Use proper techniques during sports and exercise.
  • Wear protective gear when necessary.
  • Strengthen muscles around joints to provide support.
