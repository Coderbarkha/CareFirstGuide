Ear bleeding First Aid Tutorial

Step 1: Assess the Situation

Check if the person is conscious and responsive. If they are unconscious, call for emergency help immediately.

Step 2: Control the Bleeding

Gently clean the area around the ear with a clean cloth. If there is bleeding, apply gentle pressure with a sterile bandage or cloth.

Step 3: Seek Medical Attention

If the bleeding does not stop or if there is a lot of blood, seek medical attention immediately.

Congratulations!

You have completed the Ear Bleeding First Aid Tutorial.
