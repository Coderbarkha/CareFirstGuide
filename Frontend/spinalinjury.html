A spinal injury can be life-threatening and requires immediate attention. It is crucial to handle it with care.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Pain or tenderness in the back or neck
  • Numbness or tingling in the limbs
  • Weakness or paralysis
  • Loss of bladder or bowel control

First Aid Steps

  1. Do not move the person unless they are in immediate danger.
  2. Call emergency services immediately.
  3. If trained, stabilize the head and neck.
  4. Monitor breathing and pulse until help arrives.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If there is severe pain or swelling.
  • If there is loss of sensation or movement.
  • If there are signs of shock (pale skin, rapid pulse).
