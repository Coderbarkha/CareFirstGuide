Sprains First Aid Tutorial

Step 1: Assess the Injury

Check the injured area for swelling, bruising, or deformity.

Step 2: Apply Ice

Apply ice to the injured area for 15-20 minutes every hour.

Step 3: Compression

Wrap the injured area with an elastic bandage to reduce swelling.

Step 4: Elevation

Keep the injured area elevated above the level of the heart.

Congratulations!

You have completed the sprains first aid tutorial.
