❤️ How to Treat Heart Attacks Safely

A heart attack occurs when blood flow to the heart is blocked. It can be life-threatening and requires immediate medical attention.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Pain in the arms, back, neck, jaw, or stomach

First Aid Steps

  1. Call emergency services immediately.
  2. Have the person sit down and rest.
  3. If the person is conscious, give them aspirin (if not allergic).
  4. Loosen any tight clothing.
  5. If the person becomes unconscious, start CPR.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If there are severe symptoms like chest pain or difficulty breathing.
  • If the person is unresponsive or loses consciousness.
  • If there are signs of shock (pale skin, rapid pulse).
