Muscle cramps are involuntary contractions of one or more muscles, often causing pain and discomfort.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Sudden, sharp pain in the affected muscle
  • Tightness or hardness in the muscle
  • Inability to use the affected muscle

First Aid Steps

  1. Stop any activity and rest the affected muscle.
  2. Stretch the muscle gently.
  3. Apply heat or cold to the area as needed.
  4. If cramps persist, seek medical help.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If cramps are severe or persistent.
  • If there is swelling or redness in the area.
  • If you have a history of muscle disorders.

Prevention Tips

  • Stay hydrated.
  • Stretch before and after exercise.
  • Avoid overexertion during physical activities.
