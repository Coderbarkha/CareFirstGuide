Whiplash is a neck injury caused by sudden movement of the head, often due to car accidents or falls.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Pain and stiffness in the neck
  • Headaches
  • Dizziness or blurred vision
  • Tingling or numbness in the arms

First Aid Steps

  1. Keep the person still and calm.
  2. Apply ice packs to reduce swelling.
  3. If pain is severe, seek medical help immediately.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If there is severe pain or swelling.
  • If there are signs of nerve damage (numbness, weakness).
  • If symptoms do not improve with first aid measures.
