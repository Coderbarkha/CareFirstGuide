Burns are injuries to the skin caused by heat, chemicals, electricity, or radiation.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Redness or swelling of the skin
  • Pain or tenderness in the affected area
  • Blisters or peeling skin

First Aid Steps

  1. Cool the burn under running water for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Remove any tight clothing or jewelry near the burn.
  3. Cover the burn with a sterile, non-stick bandage.
  4. Avoid applying ice directly to the burn.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If the burn is larger than 3 inches in diameter.

  • If there are signs of infection (increased pain, redness, swelling).
