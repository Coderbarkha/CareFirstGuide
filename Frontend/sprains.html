A sprain is an injury to a ligament caused by overstretching or tearing. It commonly occurs in the ankle, knee, or wrist.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Pain around the affected joint
  • Swelling and bruising
  • Limited range of motion

First Aid Steps

  1. Rest the injured area and avoid putting weight on it.
  2. Apply ice wrapped in a cloth for 15-20 minutes every hour.
  3. Compress the area with an elastic bandage to reduce swelling.
  4. Elevate the injured limb above heart level if possible.
  5. If pain persists or worsens, seek medical attention.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If there is severe pain or swelling.
  • If you cannot move the joint or bear weight on it.
  • If there are signs of a fracture (deformity, severe bruising).
