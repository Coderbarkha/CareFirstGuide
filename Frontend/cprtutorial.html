First Aid Tutorial: How to Perform CPR

Step 1: Check for responsiveness

Tap on the person’s shoulder and shout, “Are you okay?” to check if they respond.

Step 2: Call for Help

If there is no response, call emergency services immediately.

Step 3: Start Chest Compressions

Place your hands in the center of the chest and push hard and fast, at least 2 inches deep, at a rate of 100-120 compressions per minute.

Step 4: Continue CPR

Continue CPR until emergency responders arrive or the person starts breathing.
