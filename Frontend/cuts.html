Cuts are injuries that break the skin. They can be caused by sharp objects, falls, or accidents.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Visible break in the skin
  • Bleeding
  • Pain at the injury site

First Aid Steps

  1. Wash your hands before treating the cut.
  2. Rinse the cut under clean water to remove dirt and debris.
  3. Apply an antiseptic ointment to prevent infection.
  4. Cover the cut with a sterile bandage or dressing.
  5. If bleeding does not stop, seek medical help.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If the cut is deep or won't stop bleeding.
  • If there are signs of infection (redness, swelling, pus).
  • If the cut is caused by a dirty or rusty object.
