Burns Tutorial

Step 1: Assess the Burn

Check the severity of the burn. If it's a third-degree burn, seek immediate medical attention.

Step 2: Cool the Burn

Run cool (not cold) water over the burn for at least 10 minutes.

Step 3: Cover the Burn

Cover the burn with a sterile, non-stick bandage.
