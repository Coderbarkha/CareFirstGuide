❤️ How to Perform CPR Safely

Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) is a lifesaving technique used in emergencies when someone's heartbeat or breathing has stopped.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Unconsciousness
  • No breathing or only gasping
  • No pulse

First Aid Steps

  1. Call emergency services immediately.
  2. Check for responsiveness by tapping the person and shouting.
  3. If unresponsive, start chest compressions at a rate of 100-120 per minute.
  4. If trained, give rescue breaths after every 30 compressions.
  5. Continue until help arrives or the person starts breathing.

When to Seek Medical Help

  • If the person does not respond after CPR.
  • If there are signs of life but they are not fully conscious.
  • If you are unsure about the situation, always call for help.
